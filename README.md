## 📋 Project Overview
This project focuses on **Business Process Support** and **Business Intelligence (BI) Analysis**, specifically targeting the e-commerce sector. The primary goal is to transform raw product data into actionable insights that drive strategic and operational decision-making.

The analysis monitors product sales performance (sales rank and price) over a one-year period to identify trends, optimize pricing, and support long-term planning for product expansion.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
*   **Performance Monitoring:** Analyze sales rank and price fluctuations over time for key product categories (Motherboards, GPUs, Laptops).
*   **Decision Support:** Provide a data-driven foundation for both daily operational adjustments and long-term strategic initiatives.
*   **Strategic Insights:** Transform complex e-commerce datasets into clear, actionable intelligence to guide marketing and sales efforts.

---

## 🛠️ Methodology & Tools
The project follows a comprehensive data science workflow:

1.  **Data Collection:** 
    *   Utilized **Helium 10** to gather high-fidelity Amazon product data over a 12-month period.
2.  **Data Processing:** 
    *   Employed **Microsoft Excel** and **Power Query** for advanced data cleaning, normalization, and transformation.
3.  **Visualization & Dashboarding:** 
    *   Developed interactive dashboards in **Power BI**.
    *   Implemented custom **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** functions to calculate complex KPIs and performance metrics.
4.  **Predictive Modeling:** 
    *   Integrated **Orange** (Data Mining/Machine Learning tool) to forecast sales trends and predict future market behavior.

---

## 🚀 Key Insights & Results
*   **Price-Rank Dynamics:** Discovered a significant correlation between pricing strategies and organic sales rank, allowing for optimized price elasticity modeling.
*   **Seasonal Trends:** Identified peak performance windows (e.g., March–May), providing a roadmap for seasonal marketing campaigns and inventory management.
*   **Strategic Framework:** Successfully established an end-to-end BI pipeline from data acquisition to executive-level visualization.
*   **Comprehensive Evaluation:** Conducted an in-depth analysis of the social, legal, and security implications of the BI processes, ensuring compliance with data privacy standards and ethical guidelines.
